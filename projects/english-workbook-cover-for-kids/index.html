Book Cover

English Workbook Cover for Kids

A fun and educational workbook cover design for kids (Grades 1–5), visually representing phonics, letter writing, and spoken English activities.

Year :

2026

Industry :

Education

Project Duration :

1-2 Days

About the Project

This project focused on designing a cover for an English workbook (tute) targeting students from Grade 1 to Grade 5.

The workbook includes a variety of learning areas such as phonics, letter writing, spoken activities, and other foundational English skills. The cover was designed to reflect a multi-skill learning experience while remaining engaging and suitable for a wide age range of young learners.

Design Goal

The objective was to create a cover that is colorful, engaging, and informative, clearly showcasing that the workbook covers multiple aspects of English learning. It was important to design something that appeals to both younger children and slightly older primary students, while also giving a sense of structure and educational value.

Creative Direction

The design uses bright and balanced colors, playful yet readable typography, and carefully selected visual elements to represent different learning activities like phonics and writing.

The layout was structured to maintain clarity and organization, ensuring that multiple topics can be visually represented without making the design feel cluttered. The overall style blends fun visuals with a structured academic look.

Tools & My Role

This project was created using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.

I was responsible for the full design process, including concept development, visual selection, layout design, and final composition. The focus was on delivering a design that is both visually appealing and educationally meaningful for young learners.
